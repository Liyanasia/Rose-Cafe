About us

Welcome to the Rose Cafe, where we take pride in serving the most delicious breakfast and pastries around. At our cafe, we understand that a good day starts with a great breakfast, and that's why we go above and beyond to create the perfect dining experience for our customers.
The moment you step into the rose-themed cafe, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the sight of vibrant, blooming roses take your breath away. The ambiance is warm and inviting, with cozy seating areas and elegant decor that exude a sense of comfort and relaxation. The menu features an array of delectable dishes, including signature rose-infused desserts that are as beautiful to look at as they are delicious. From the fluffy pancakes with rose petals to the fragrant rose tea, every dish is carefully crafted to provide a memorable dining experience. As you savor your meal, you can't help but feel surrounded by love and warmth, as if you're enjoying a meal in a cozy, intimate setting with friends and family

Dining Experience

Dining is not just about the food, it's about the experience. From the moment you step inside our doors, you'll be transported to a world of beauty, elegance, and sophistication. Our unique and romantic atmosphere is the perfect setting for a special occasion, or for simply enjoying a relaxing meal with friends and loved ones.

Location

Rose Cafe is located in the bustling city of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Specifically, it is situated in the elegant area of City Walk, a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. The cafe's address is Building 6, City Walk, Al Wasl Road, Dubai, making it easily accessible from all parts of the city. The location of the Rose Cafe offers visitors a unique experience of dining in a stylish and sophisticated ambiance, surrounded by modern architecture and a vibrant atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a quick bite, a relaxing afternoon tea or a full-blown meal, the Rose Cafe's central location in Dubai makes it a convenient and must-visit destination for foodies and coffee lovers.
